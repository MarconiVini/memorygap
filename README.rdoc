# Memorygap

> People are dumb, and they(specially me) forgot their passwords
> and by that time Memorygap seemed to be quite awesome solution!

This is a small project for storing any kinds of key-value secure string. 
Whether they are names, dates, passwords or numbers, they will be securely hidden into our servers.

## Usage 
This is a open-software and virtually anyone can use it in your own server, with your own risks.

Enjoy!
